The agro-sensor-cloud is a unique platform, where multiple farmers/users can get different agricultural services such as irrigation management, crop health monitoring, and crop security. Additionally, in this system, there is a provision to get raw data of different environmental parameters, which include temperature, humidity, rainfall, and moisture. The agro-sensor-cloud system consists of a Web-based user-friendly interface through which the users can easily monitor the situations of a particular field. The system is based on the pay-per-use model, and therefore, a user needs to pay for the services based on the usage. The users are completely abstracted from the backend architecture, equipment, and the logic used for particular services. The agro-sensor-cloud primarily provides three types of services--Irrigation, Crop-Health, and Crop-Security.

Why SWAN Sensor Cloud?

The farmers need to monitor different agricultural parameters such as soil moisture and water-level for better farming. However, in such monitoring process, the farmers need to procure and deploy the sensor nodes, which are appropriate for sensing the data of respective parameters. Moreover, the crop destruction is one of the major issues for the farmers. The crop may be destructed either by the animals or fire. Consequently, for monitoring the crop destruction, a farmer needs to procure expensive cameras or set-up an alert generation system in the field. Additionally, the farmers must have a fair experience in different aspects (such as which system will be appropriate for his/her field, from which vendor the system should be procured, the correct deployment and maintenance of the system) in purchasing the expensive equipment and systems. The Agro-Sensor-Cloud system abolishes the burden of the farmers by providing different technological solutions to monitor the farming.

Dynamic
